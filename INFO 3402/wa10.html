Introduction

Transitions provide the author with a way to better engage the audience that view's their content. Transitions surged in the 2000s with Microsoft PowerPoint with some absolutely rediculous ways of moving from slide to slide. Click to the next slide to see one of these.

Statement of Problem

Slide transitions can be implemented by using the tag data-transition in the section tag. Transitions may not always work perfectly on every device and can also distract from the content if too overwhelming.

Different Effects

These effects can be applied to your slides:

  • none: changes instantly
  • fade: cross fade
  • slide: slides between backgrounds
  • convex: slide at a convex angle
  • concave: slide at a concave angle
  • zoom: scale the incoming slide up so it grows in from the center of the screen
